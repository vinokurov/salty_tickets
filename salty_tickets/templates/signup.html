Terms and Conditions

  1. We, Salty Jitterbugs Limited, are receiving money from you to cover the organisational expenses within the following event: {{ event.name }}.
  2. We are not making any profit for ourselves. All of the received money will be spent to cover the weekend expenses.
  3. Unless your registration is in the waiting list, your payment guarantees you the place in the selected workshop/party with the selected dance role.
Waiting lists
  1. If any of the items in your order are marked as a "Waiting List", this means that your purchaase doesn't give you access to the workshop/party immediately. Instead you are put on the waiting list
  2. Waiting lists are applied when there are too many sign up requests from leaders or followers and the class becomes unbalanced, when the total capacity of the event is reached, or when event accepts only partner registrations.
  3. Once the balance gets back to normal or if someone cancels and there are more available places, we move registrations off the waiting lists to populate the available places.
  4. Waiting lists are processed automatically in the order of registration date and time. I.e. those who signed up earlier get off the waiting list first.
  5. Registrations in the waiting list have higher priority than the new sign ups. This means that if we get available places we will clear waiting lists as much as possible before allowing new sign ups to take the available places.
  6. Those who sign up with a partner don't get in the waiting list if there are available places in the event.
  7. If you are in the waiting list you can get off it if you find a partner. For this give your registration token to your partner. Note that your partner needs to be of an opposite role and you should be signing up for the same workshop.
  8. Once you get off the waiting list you will be notified by email.
  9. Why do you need to pay even if you are in the waiting list?
    • This allows us to process waiting lists automatically, in the most fair order and without any delays caused by waiting for payments.
  10. You can cancel your place in the waiting list any time and get refunded.
  11. If event starts and you didn't get off the waiting list, you will be refunded.
Refunds
  1. We guarantee 100% refund only if your registration is in the waiting list. In other cases we will see what we can do.
  2. Refunds are processed after we manually review your cancellation request.
  3. The money are returned approximately 5-10 business days later, depending on your bank.
  4. Money can be refunded back only to the bank account you used for the registration.
  5. If you had any discounts and you are refunding your order partially the refunded amount will be recalculated. For example, there are 2 workshops Â£30 each or Â£50 for both. Signing up with a partner
  1. You can sign up with a partner by buying both tickets in one purchase. Use "Book for myself" and "Book for partner" buttons.
  2. Another way to sign up with a partner which allows you to pay separately is to use the registration token which is sent by email.

    • in the registration confirmation email he/she will receive tokens for every workshop in his/her order,
