I yam what I yam and tha's all what I yam! Do you follow a properly balanced Collegiate Shag diet? Meet the chefs of our next Salty Recipes: Patrick O'Brien and Fancy Dougherty. Like Popeye the Sailor and Olive Oyl they will teach us magic nutrition secrets that make their style so smooth & elegant but so energetic that not even Brutus could be able to fight against that force.

There will be two Shag recipes to satisfy the most extravagant tastes: "Olive oil - smooth your Shag" and "Spinach - boost your Shag" focused on smooth, fast elegant technique and energetic moves and combos.

The level of the workshops is improvers/intermediate, assuming you can socially dance Shag, comfortable with the footwork and basic figures. You don't need a partner to sign up, however signing up together will let you avoid the waiting list if there is one.

To give you a perfect experience during the workshop we will limit the capacity to just 25 places and will limit the balance between leads and followers.

As a gratitude to those who supported our first Salty Recipes we offer a special discount price to all of the attendants of the recipes with Pol & Sara. Also there is a discount for the first 15 registered and a discount for those who book both olive oil and spinach recipes.
